Our verification process is simple yet secure. You can verify certificates through three methods: 1) Enter the certificate ID provided on the document, 2) Scan the QR code printed on the certificate, or 3) Upload the PDF certificate for hash comparison. Our system instantly checks the blockchain for a matching record and confirms authenticity.

Certificate data is stored with cryptographic protection, and our systems undergo regular security audits. We never store sensitive personal information unnecessarily.

Getting started is easy! If you're an educational institution looking to issue certificates, simply create an account, complete the verification process, and you can begin issuing blockchain-secured certificates. If you're an employer or individual looking to verify a certificate, no account is needed - just use our verification tools on the homepage.
